Perfect Autum Treat: Batter Cakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup thick cream (enough flour, to make a stiff batter)
  • 2 egg, yolks
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 egg, whites
  • salt

Directions

  1. Add flour to thick cream to make a stiff batter
  2. Beat thoroughly
  3. Add beaten yolks of two eggs and baking soda
  4. Mix well, salting slightly
  5. Toss in beaten whites and bake on a griddle


You can also follow this picture tutorial. After all of the steps, whisk and cook on the stovetop as desired.
